What is The Garden of Sinners

The Garden of Sinners, known in Japan as Kara no Kyōkai (空の境界, lit. "Boundary of Emptiness") and sometimes referred to as Rakkyo (らっきょ), is a Japanese light novel series, written by Kinoko Nasu and illustrated by Takashi Takeuchi.

The Garden of Sinners story

Story. Kara no Kyōkai follows the story of Shiki Ryōgi, a teenage girl raised as a demon hunter who acquired the "Mystic Eyes of Death Perception" after surviving a fatal accident.
